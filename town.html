My Town

I am from a small town in Connecticut called Prospect. It is a pretty small suburban town with just around a population of 10,000 people. It was one of the newest towns created in Connecticut mainly because it was on a hill and there was no water there unless you dug deep into the ground using wells. It used to be considered the highest town in Connecticut based on elevation but that was later debunked. It is a very quiet small town where not much happens. We have a mayor who has been mayor since 1977 making him one of the longest serving mayors in the whole country. Nothing much happens in this town but we have a town Facebook page where people act like it is their own personal page and not one shared by the whole town.
